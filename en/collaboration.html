Participating Institutions

At present, the team consists of many universities and research institutions in China, including Tsinghua University, Xiamen University, Tongji University, Nanjing University, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, University of Science and Technology of China, Shanghai Astronomical Observatory (SHAO), Technical Institution of Physics and Chemistry (IPC), Shanghai Institution of Microsystem and Information Technology (SIMIT), Purple Mountain Observatory (PMO) and Shanghai Academy of Spaceflight Technology (SAST), as well as international collaborators, including SRON in the Netherlands, University of Wisconsin-Madison and University of Massachusetts. Many more institutions have expressed strong interest.
